About Me

Our world is quickly moving towards more distinct, specialized and accessible spaces - with our screens, and the content behind them, being at the forefront of this evolution. The way content is built, created, and interfaced with can be a make or break experience for users, and this is why I believe in creating streamlined, focused experiences as an important part of website creation & platform integrations. Drawing from my education in psychology, I am passionate about building things that facilitate connection in their design, and welcoming people into their spaces. Displaying content in a meaningful way that makes sense to a user can open all kinds of information pathways, and creating pages with this in mind is key. Although most of my coding experience is with full-stack apps, I am best utilized and most effective as a front-end developer. Being a visual learner and diagnosed with ADHD, I understand the importance of well designed websites and streamlined user experiences. Design simplicity to allow for maximum information access is key, and utilizing my own experiences I feel I have a lot to contribute in website development along these lines. I am currently seeking full time employment with a company who knows its investment in me will not go undeserved. Though I would love to work more with UX/UI designers on this next leg of my journey, I am also open to additional experiences that fit the values of creating streamlined and intuitive experiences that I bring to the table.
